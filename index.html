Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What is home physiotherapy?

Home physiotherapy brings professional physiotherapy services to the comfort of your home. Our certified physiotherapists provide personalized treatment plans tailored to your specific needs, ensuring effective rehabilitation without the need to travel to a clinic.

What conditions do you treat?

We treat a wide range of conditions including back pain, neck pain, joint pain, sports injuries, post-surgery rehabilitation, neurological conditions, pediatric physiotherapy, respiratory issues, and geriatric care. Our comprehensive services cater to patients of all ages and conditions.

Are your physiotherapists certified?

Yes, all our physiotherapists are certified professionals with specialized training and years of experience in various physiotherapy specializations. They undergo continuous professional development to stay updated with the latest treatment techniques and best practices.

What areas in Pune do you cover?

We provide home physiotherapy services across all major areas in Pune including Koregaon Park, Viman Nagar, Kharadi, Hadapsar, Baner, Wakad, Hinjewadi, Aundh, Shivaji Nagar, Camp Area, and surrounding localities. Contact us to confirm service availability in your specific area.

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by filling out the booking form on our website, calling us directly, or sending us a message via WhatsApp. Our team will assist you in scheduling a convenient time and date for your home physiotherapy session.

What should I expect during a home physiotherapy session?

During a home physiotherapy session, our physiotherapist will conduct a thorough assessment of your condition, discuss your treatment goals, and develop a personalized treatment plan. The session may include manual therapy, exercise prescription, pain management techniques, and education on self-care strategies.

What are your safety protocols for home visits?

We adhere to strict safety protocols for all home visits. Our physiotherapists wear masks, use hand sanitizers, and follow hygiene guidelines to ensure a safe and comfortable environment for our patients. We also conduct regular health checks for our staff to prevent any potential risks.

What equipment do you bring for a home session?

Our physiotherapists bring all necessary equipment for a comprehensive physiotherapy session, including exercise bands, weights, massage tools, and other therapeutic devices. We ensure that all equipment is clean and well-maintained for optimal treatment outcomes.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule my appointment?

If you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment, please notify us at least 24 hours in advance. We will do our best to accommodate your request and find an alternative time that works for you. Late cancellations may be subject to a cancellation fee.
